My life

This page is dedicated to my personal life, find out what I do in my spare time, my passions and activities.

Sustainability and civics engagement

I am very involved in serving and giving back to my community. My priority at the moment is fighting overconsumption and fast fashion.

Second-hand clothing

In an effort to reduce my carbon footprint I set a goal for myself: to not purchase new clothing. Therefore, for the past 4 years I have exclusively shopped at local thrift stores: Emaus in France, Goodwill, The Salvation Army and local benefit shops in the U.S. or on online second hand websites like Vinted, Depop or Ebay.

Engagement at ENSEEIHT

I joined ENSEEIHT's charity work club, Human7. We will be participating and organizing various activities like the Octobre Rose run for cancer, humanitarian trips to Madagascar, Togo, Chile and Nepal as well as getting involved and helping out the community right here in Toulouse.
