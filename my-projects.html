TIPE (travaux d'initiative personnelle encadrés):

As part of the entrance exams for engineering school we were required to solve a problem on the topic of environmental transition, conversion and transformation. Alongside two of my classmates we decided to tackle problems relating to solar panels, particularly how to optimise their power production. The solution we chose was a maximum power point tracking algorithm which overrides external factors such as temperature, irradiance and orientation to at the point of maximum power output.
