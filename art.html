These past few years, as time flies by quicker than ever before, painting has truly become a therapy for me. It allows me to disconnect from reality and get lost in a creative realm where I am no longer rushed by time. Alongside difficult and very technical studies I find it crucial to have a hobby that shuts your brain off completely. I have hopes of pursuing art later in life as well in whichever form that may look like —whether it be creating, collecting, or teaching.
